The six-stage development pipeline

Stage 1 — Concept & Target Definition

We define each product’s purpose — be it cognitive clarity, hormonal balance, or immune modulation — and align it with both Ayurvedic insights (e.g., dosha imbalances) and modern biomedical targets (e.g., CGRP, cortisol, TAC, homocysteine).

Stage 2 — Phytochemical Profiling & Quantum Alignment

Using electron microscopy (EM), nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR), and AI-powered platforms like NutrifyGenie, we identify potent bioactive molecules and assess their synergistic energy alignment. This ensures each herb combination is efficacious at a molecular level and grounded in classical pairings.

Stage 3 — Prototype Formulation

We develop high-bioavailability prototypes that translate the validated phytochemistry into stable, deliverable actives. All ingredients meet clean-label standards and are screened for environmental sustainability.

Stage 4 — Efficacy & Safety Testing

Our in vitro testing screens prototypes for biological activity and tolerability before they ever reach a human study. We also assess ingredient stability, safety profiles, and formulation integrity under GMP standards.

Stage 5 — Pilot Clinical Validation

Each validated prototype undergoes biomarker-based human pilot studies — for example, CGRP reduction, total antioxidant capacity (TAC), skin glow index, and energy/fatigue scales. Adjustments are made based on both data and user response.

  • Biomarker-based human pilot studies (e.g., CGRP reduction, TAC, skin glow index, energy/fatigue scales)
  • Adjustments made based on both objective data and real-world user response

Stage 6 — Launch & Regulatory Compliance

Formulations are released only after rigorous evaluation and documentation. Our launch strategy spans HCP recommendations, digital wellness platforms, and global market-ready models.

  • Efficacy and tolerability evaluations using global gold standards
  • Packaging aligned with eco-labels and traceability systems
  • Clinical documentation and transparent label disclosures
