Formulation Expert — Herbal / Nutraceutical / Cosmetics

Job summary

We are seeking a skilled and innovative Formulation Expert with over 12–15 years of experience in the development of herbal, nutraceutical, or cosmetic formulations. The ideal candidate will be responsible for end-to-end formulation development, from ideation to scale-up and commercial transfer.

Key responsibilities

  • Develop and optimize novel formulations and product concepts based on project requirements.
  • Develop and optimize formulations for herbal extracts, nutraceuticals, tablets, capsules, syrups, creams, and cosmetics.
  • Conduct literature research, pre-formulation trials, and stability studies.
  • Collaborate with R&D, QC, and production teams for smooth technology transfer.
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory standards (FSSAI, AYUSH, WHO-GMP).
  • Prepare detailed documentation — formulation records, batch manufacturing records, etc.
  • Innovate and improve existing product lines based on market and technical feedback.
  • Evaluate new raw materials and ingredients for functional and formulation compatibility.
  • Stay updated with market trends, scientific advancements, and formulation techniques.

Key requirements

  • 12–15 years of hands-on formulation experience in the herbal / nutraceutical / cosmetic industry.
  • Strong knowledge of herbal actives, excipients, and processing parameters.
  • Experience with dosage forms like tablets, capsules, liquids, powders, and topicals.
  • Good documentation and data interpretation skills.
  • Familiar with regulatory documentation and dossier preparation.
  • Exposure to stability protocols and scale-up processes.

Preferred skills

  • Knowledge of natural preservatives, bioenhancers, and delivery systems.
  • Experience working in a GMP / GLP-compliant setup.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel).
  • Project planning skills — desirable / good to have.
