Job description

What you’ll do.

We are seeking a dynamic and committed HR Executive to manage the complete employee lifecycle and support HR operations at GreenSpace Herbs. The role involves handling statutory requirements and may require travel to meet labor officials, government departments, and external vendors.

⚖
Statutory compliance ownership

Keep PF, ESI, Gratuity, and Shops & Establishments obligations current and audit-ready.

✓
Full-spectrum HR exposure

From recruitment to exits — touch every stage of the employee lifecycle.

Key responsibilities

Seven areas of ownership.

A people-driven role spanning recruitment, lifecycle management, compliance, payroll support, engagement, reporting, and on-ground statutory work.

★

1. Recruitment & Onboarding

Source candidates through portals, referrals, and consultants; schedule and coordinate interviews with department heads; prepare offer letters, appointment letters, and onboarding kits; and conduct joining formalities and employee orientation.

♻

2. Employee Lifecycle Management

Maintain employee records in ERP and physical files; manage confirmations, transfers, promotions, resignations, and exits; and conduct exit interviews and process full & final settlements.

⚖

3. HR Operations & Compliance

Ensure timely updating and compliance with statutory norms — PF, ESI, Gratuity, and the Shops & Establishments Act; handle labor office visits and liaise with government officials; and maintain registers and ensure timely returns submission.

⏱

4. Attendance, Leave & Payroll Support

Monitor biometric attendance, leave records, and late logins; coordinate with the accounts/payroll team for salary processing; and manage holiday lists, shift schedules, and leave encashments.

♥

5. Employee Engagement & Communication

Support HR initiatives such as rewards & recognition, birthday celebrations, and internal communications that keep the team connected and motivated.

▤

6. Documentation & Reporting

Prepare HR MIS reports, dashboards, and trackers that give leadership clear visibility into people metrics and operational health.

⚲

7. Travel for Statutory Work

Visit labor offices, PF/ESIC offices, and other government departments when required; and coordinate with consultants and external agencies for compliance audits.
