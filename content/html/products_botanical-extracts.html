Why standardization matters

Consistency is the precondition for clinical performance.

A defined marker percentage is what separates a reproducible nutraceutical ingredient from a variable botanical powder. Our extracts are built around four guarantees.

⌖

Defined markers

Each extract is held to a specified active-compound percentage, declared on every certificate of analysis.

⚗

Validated assays

Marker content is confirmed with validated chromatographic and spectroscopic methods on every batch.

🛡

Purity & safety

Heavy-metal, microbial and residual-solvent limits are controlled — Garcinia, for instance, at Lead < 0.5 ppm.

♻

Traceable sourcing

Identity-verified botanicals from controlled supply chains, manufactured under audited GMP systems.
