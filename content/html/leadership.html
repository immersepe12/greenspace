Meet the team

Distinguished minds in quantum chemistry and Ayurveda.

Decades of combined expertise in formulation chemistry, natural product science, molecular modelling and clinical research.

Hirehal Nuruddin Shafiulla

Managing Director · Founder · Quantum Ayurveda Innovator

At the helm of GreenSpace Herbs' scientific journey, Shafiulla bridges engineering, chemistry and integrative medicine. His fascination with the isolation of bioactive compounds and their subatomic, energetic interactions led to the development of Quantum Ayurveda. Rather than chasing isolated actives, he champions synergistic plant matrices designed for long-term health and sustainable wellness — a synthesis of scientific discipline and visionary thought.

Tahira H S

Chief Research Officer · Formulator & Product Visionary

A passionate champion of botanicals and timeless Ayurvedic wisdom, Tahira stands at the forefront of GreenSpace Herbs' product innovation. She leads formulation with integrity — meticulously selecting raw materials and working closely with Ayurvedic scholars, clinicians and researchers to drive evidence-based innovation. Her leadership bridges the sacred precision of Ayurveda with the expectations of the modern wellness consumer.

Dr. Anil Kumar Kruthiventi

Professor of Practice · Center for Drug Design, CUTM · Natural Product Chemistry

A veteran researcher with 30+ years of experience in nutraceuticals, preventive healthcare and nano-biotechnology. He has authored three books, published over 60 papers and holds 23 patents. Formerly with Dr. Reddy's Institute of Life Sciences and Tata Chemicals Ltd., he led R&D and launched new verticals in Prebiotics (FOS) and Nano Silica, and today advises industry and academia on natural product innovation.

Dr. Anand Solomon

Ph.D in Chemistry · Phytochemical Researcher · Crystallography

A natural products chemist with 25 years of experience in phytochemical research, solid-state formulations and crystal engineering. He holds multiple patents on solubility-enhancing co-crystals of herbal actives such as embelin and forskolin. With 50+ publications and leadership of funded projects on nutraceutical innovations, he combines traditional plant science with modern materials chemistry.
