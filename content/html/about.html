Who We Are

Greenspace Herbs is a next-generation herbal therapeutics company dedicated to bridging the timeless efficacy of Ayurveda with the precision of modern biomedical sciences. Positioned at the forefront of plant-based healthcare, we specialize in developing holistic, clinically supported botanical ingredients designed to address chronic lifestyle disorders, restore balance, and support long-term well-being.

By integrating deep Ayurvedic understanding with contemporary technologies such as AI modeling, Raman Spectroscopy and molecular docking, our work under the Quantum Ayurveda™ framework is setting new standards in evidence-based natural medicine.

What We Do

At Greenspace Herbs, we transform the vast potential of herbal pharmacology through the lens of Quantum Ayurveda by integrating ancient wisdom with modern science to create purpose-built health solutions that are validated, target specific, and energetically aligned.

Our portfolio features energized formulations designed to support hormonal equilibrium, neuro-adaptogen support, gut detoxification, inflammatory resolution, and metabolic reprogramming. Each product is developed to resonate at optimal frequencies enhancing cellular receptivity and therapeutic impact.

Our teams of Ayurvedic experts, phytochemists, and clinical researchers collaborate to ensure every product is rooted in traditional knowledge, refined with phytochemical accuracy, and delivered through mechanisms that maximize bioavailability, efficacy, safety mechanisms and frequency-specific activation.

Timeline of Growth & Transformation

Laying the Foundation

Greenspace Herbs began as a contract manufacturer focused on pure, high-quality herbal extracts. Greenspace Herbs built strong technical expertise in extraction processes and plant pharmacognosy.

2006
2010

Research Lab Expansion

Established first in-house Quality Control (QC) laboratory and started exploring high value actives. Proprietary extraction processes and standardization methods became part of core capabilities.

The Formulation Leap

Expansion from herbal extracts to outcome oriented solutions and moved into formulation science. This led to the development of finished personal care products such as "Pain & Stress Body Massage Oil"; "Respiratory Wellness", "baby oil" and "hair oil" formulations.

2012
2017

Investing in Innovation

Expanded facilities to meet growing global demand and investing in next-generation herbal actives. Examples include Garcinia cambogia extracts with lead content <0.5 ppm, high-grade Coleus forskohlii, and Boswellia serrata standardized to 80% boswellic acids. These advances laid the groundwork for more targeted, bioavailable, and clinically validated formulations.
