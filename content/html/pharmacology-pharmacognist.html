Pharmacology / Pharmacognosist

We are seeking a qualified and motivated M.Pharm (Pharmacology) professional to support pharmacological research, preclinical studies and scientific documentation — driving experimental planning, data analysis and regulatory-grade rigour within our R&D team.

Job summary

This role involves experimental planning, data analysis, literature review and compliance with regulatory and ethical standards. You will work hands-on across in vitro and in vivo studies, translating sound scientific methodology into clearly documented, defensible results that support our botanical, API and formulation research.

Key responsibilities

  • Conduct pharmacological and preclinical studies (in vitro and/or in vivo) as per study protocols.
  • Assist in study design, execution and interpretation of results.
  • Perform data collection, statistical analysis and report preparation.
  • Review and compile scientific literature related to drugs, APIs, herbal products or formulations.
  • Prepare and maintain study reports, SOPs, protocols and documentation.
  • Ensure compliance with GLP, GCP and ethical guidelines.
  • Support regulatory submissions and responses, if required.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams such as R&D, QA, Regulatory Affairs and Clinical teams.
  • Maintain laboratory instruments, records and safety standards.

Required skills & knowledge

  • Strong understanding of pharmacodynamics and pharmacokinetics.
  • Knowledge of drug screening, toxicity studies and safety pharmacology.
  • Ability to analyze data and prepare technical and scientific reports.
  • Good knowledge of research methodologies and statistics.
  • Proficiency in MS Office and basic data analysis tools.

Preferred skills (added advantage)

  • Experience in animal handling and experimental models.
  • Exposure to herbal / nutraceutical / pharmaceutical research.
  • Knowledge of regulatory requirements (ICH, CDSCO, US FDA — basic).
