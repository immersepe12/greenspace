Personal Assistant Executive

We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Personal Assistant Executive to provide comprehensive administrative and executive support to senior management at GreenSpace Herbs.

Role overview

We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Personal Assistant Executive to provide comprehensive administrative and executive support to senior management. The role requires excellent communication, multitasking abilities, and discretion in handling sensitive information.

Reporting directly to the Managing Director, you will act as the primary point of contact between executives and internal and external stakeholders — keeping schedules, correspondence, and follow-ups running smoothly in a fast-paced environment.

Key responsibilities

  • Manage and maintain executive schedules, calendars, and appointments.
  • Coordinate and arrange meetings, conferences, and travel itineraries.
  • Draft, review, and manage correspondence, emails, and official communications.
  • Prepare reports, presentations, and documentation as required.
  • Handle confidential and sensitive information with utmost integrity.
  • Act as the primary point of contact between executives and internal/external stakeholders.
  • Assist in monitoring deadlines, tasks, and follow-ups to ensure smooth workflow.
  • Support executives in personal tasks, including travel, event coordination, and errands, when required.
  • Maintain filing systems (electronic and physical) for efficient document management.
  • Perform other administrative and support duties as assigned by management.

Requirements

  • Proven experience (2–5 years) as a Personal Assistant, Executive Assistant, or similar role.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Strong organizational, time management, and multitasking abilities.
  • Proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) and digital collaboration tools.
  • Ability to work independently and handle pressure in a fast-paced environment.
  • High level of professionalism, discretion, and confidentiality.

Qualifications

Education: Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Commerce, Arts, or a related field.

Additional skills

  • Positive attitude with problem-solving skills.
  • Attention to detail and ability to anticipate needs.
  • Flexible and adaptable to changing priorities.
  • Strong interpersonal skills for effective interaction with internal teams and external partners.

What we offer

  • Opportunities for career growth and professional development.
  • Supportive and collaborative work environment.
