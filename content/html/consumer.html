The present era debates the use of a standardized herbal extract versus a whole herb in the final product. Evidence-based literature indicates that an extract equivalent in ingredient composition ratios to the whole herb is enriched with phytochemical constituents — superior active principles that allow flexibility in administering the required dose.

Why standardized extracts

More of the good stuff, in every dose.

A standardized extract concentrates the active principles of a botanical while preserving the composition ratios of the whole herb. The result is a phytochemically enriched ingredient — consistent batch to batch, easier to dose precisely, and validated for real therapeutic effect.

Bio-assays are the ultimate tool to establish therapeutic efficacy, and every GreenSpace extract is profiled and quality-assured before it ever reaches a formulation.
