The idea in one line

From a good plant to a proven active.

Traditional botanicals work — but their strength swings with sourcing, processing and how well the body absorbs them. AYUQUANTA 7™ removes that guesswork. It runs every botanical through the same seven checkpoints, so what comes out the other end is consistent, measurable and backed by data.

The output is a new category of ingredient — an Energized Active Supplement Ingredient (EASI)™ — the same botanical, made more responsive in the body, with each claim tied to a measurement.

Input

A standardized, GMP-grade Ayurvedic extract.

The process

7 steps · 3 phases · guided by AI, confirmed by spectroscopy.

Output

A clinically validated EASI™ energized active.

The framework

Seven steps, three phases.

Each step feeds the next. We've grouped them into three plain-language phases so the whole journey reads in a glance — purify the botanical, design the active, then energize it and prove it works.

Phase 1 · Purify
01

Start with a clean, consistent extract

Every batch begins in a GMP-certified facility, where sourcing, processing and quality control are monitored end-to-end. Purity and batch-to-batch consistency are built in from the first step — not inspected for at the end.

STEP OUTPUT · a pure, standardized botanical extract
Phase 2 · Design
02

Let AI find the best version

Our discovery AI, NutrifyGenie, sifts through molecular data to surface the strongest bioactives and blends — combinations and synergies that would be impractical to find by hand.

STEP OUTPUT · an AI-recommended active or blend
03

Validate it on a computer first

Before any physical work, computer-aided design (CADD) models how the candidate behaves — checking how it fits its targets and flagging issues early, so only the strongest options move forward.

STEP OUTPUT · a modelled, de-risked candidate
04

Capture its unique signature

A Raman spectrometer records the ingredient's baseline molecular fingerprint — a precise reference we can measure against later to prove nothing drifted.

STEP OUTPUT · a baseline spectral fingerprint
Phase 3 · Energize & Prove
05

Load the energy

In tuned energy chambers, the ingredient is primed to hold a small, calibrated charge — the step that turns a standard extract into an energized active.

STEP OUTPUT · an energy-loaded active
06

Re-check the signature

A second Raman scan confirms the loading worked and that the fingerprint matches the intended target — the proof of reproducibility before anything ships to a trial.

STEP OUTPUT · a verified, reproducible energy state
07

Prove it in people

Finally, ethically conducted human studies test the finished active for real-world effect and safety — the highest bar of validation, and the reason every EASI™ claim rests on evidence.

STEP OUTPUT · a clinically validated EASI™ active
