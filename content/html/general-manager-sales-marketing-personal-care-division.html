General Manager — Sales & Marketing (Personal Care Division).

A strategic, result-oriented leader to drive revenue growth, expand market presence and strengthen brand positioning across domestic and international markets.

Position Overview

We are looking for a strategic and result-oriented General Manager — Sales & Marketing to lead our Personal Care product division. The role involves driving revenue growth, expanding market presence, strengthening brand positioning, and building a high-performing sales and marketing team across domestic and international markets.

Role & Responsibilities

1. Sales Strategy & Planning

  • Design and implement long-term and short-term sales strategies aligned with organizational goals.
  • Develop annual operating plans including revenue projections, sales forecasting, and budget allocation.
  • Define monthly, quarterly, and yearly sales targets and ensure structured tracking mechanisms.

2. Market & Channel Development

  • Identify high-growth markets, new geographies, and emerging consumer segments.
  • Drive B2B business through distributors, institutional sales, contract manufacturing partnerships, and bulk buyers.
  • Expand B2C presence across retail chains, modern trade, general trade, and e-commerce platforms.
  • Strengthen export sales and international distribution networks.
  • Develop customized commercial models for different sales channels.

3. Sales Operations & Performance

  • Lead nationwide sales operations ensuring target achievement and profitability.
  • Monitor primary and secondary sales performance with structured review systems.
  • Optimize pricing frameworks, margin structures, and trade schemes.
  • Ensure disciplined sales MIS reporting and performance analytics.

4. Brand & Marketing Leadership

  • Establish strong brand identity and differentiated positioning in the personal care segment.
  • Direct new product launches including go-to-market strategies.
  • Oversee packaging development and ensure market-aligned presentation standards.

5. Key Account & Trade Management

  • Develop strategic relationships with major retail chains, distributors, and online marketplaces.
  • Lead commercial negotiations and finalize high-value contracts.
  • Strengthen trade marketing execution and in-store visibility programs.

6. Demand Planning & Cross-Functional Integration

  • Align sales forecasts with production and supply chain planning to ensure optimal inventory levels.
  • Collaborate with R&D and QA/QC for product performance enhancements and regulatory alignment.
  • Ensure smooth coordination between finance and sales for budget control and profitability tracking.

7. Team Leadership & Development

  • Build, mentor, and scale a high-performing sales and marketing team.
  • Establish measurable KPIs and conduct structured performance evaluations.

Qualifications & Experience

  • MBA in Marketing / Sales / Business Administration (preferred).
  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or a related discipline.
  • 20+ years of progressive experience in Sales & Marketing.
  • At least 5 years in a senior leadership capacity.
  • Mandatory exposure to Personal Care / FMCG / Cosmetics / Consumer Products sector (Herbal industry preferred).
